1. 

Opening address – Mr. Tom Enright, Chief Executive 

Mr. T. Enright advised that the 2024 budget reflects a 9% increase on 2023. Mr. Enright 
said the increase reflects the increased cost of doing business and increase in service 
provision. Mr. Enright advised that the budget will provide for high quality services in 2024.

Decision on Rates to be applied to Vacant Premises 

Recommendation to retain tiered basis.  
•  Retain 90% relief for all properties with annual rates to €2,000 
•  Apply 80% relief for properties with annual rates over €2,000 and less than €10,000 
•  Apply 60% relief for properties with annual rates over €10,000 

Cllr. T. Forde proposed a reduction of 10% on each of the three tiers of the Rates Abatement 
Scheme (new rates to be 50%, 70% & 80%). This was seconded by Cllr. F. Ó'Súilleabháin. 

The Cathaoirleach advised that there would be a roll call vote in relation to Cllr. Forde’s 
proposed amendment to the Rates Abatement Scheme. The outcome was that the proposal to 
amend the Rates Abatement Scheme was defeated with 8 in favour and 20 against. The voting 
was as follows:

On foot of the adoption of the proposed tiered rates abatement, Wexford County Council 
makes a scheme for the abatement of rates due to it by liable persons, or classes of liable 
persons, in respect of vacant properties in accordance with the provisions of Section 9 of the 
Local Government Rates and Other Matters Act 2019, as amended. Such a scheme in respect 
of vacant property will provide for an abatement of either 60%, 80% or 90% (depending on 
value of annual rates) of rates due to Wexford County Council by a liable person for the 
financial year ending 31 December 2024. 

Adoption of Annual Rate on Valuation (ARV) 
Recommendation – Retain ARV of €0.253  

On the proposal of Cllr. B.A. Murphy, seconded by Cllr. G. Carthy and agreed by all, the 
Members resolved to retain the ARV of €0.253. 

Decision on Small Business Support Scheme 
Recommendation - retain existing scheme 

➢  To provide SBSG at 10% of Annual Rates up to a max of €800 
➢  To be applied to all with annual rates up to €30,000 meeting T&C’s 

On the proposal of Cllr. G. Carthy, seconded by Cllr. M. Whelan and agreed by all, the 
Members resolved to retain the existing Small Business Support Scheme. 

Adopt 2024 Budget with or without amendment 

On the proposal of Cllr. B.A. Murphy, seconded by Cllr. O. Walsh and agreed by all, the 
Members resolved to adopt the 2024 budget with an amendment to increase the Amenity 
Grants by €1,000 per Member (€7,000 to €8,000).
